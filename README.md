# BluePath — Smart Ocean AI Learning Platform

BluePath is a mobile learning platform that helps people of different ages explore the ocean, build practical knowledge, discover marine careers, and grow through a visible achievement journey. It brings curated learning content, promotion quizzes, personalized guidance, schedules, reminders, and a source-grounded AI companion into one ocean-inspired experience.

## Why BluePath Matters

Ocean education often feels scattered across videos, institutional programs, career information, and qualification resources. BluePath connects those pieces into a single learning path.

- **Learners know where to begin.** A short ocean-talent diagnosis identifies interests, goals, age group, and current level.
- **Every activity contributes to progress.** Video completion, quizzes, saved opportunities, and advanced projects become part of a visible growth record.
- **Learning leads to real possibilities.** Marine topics are connected with NCS competencies, job roles, training pathways, and relevant workplaces.
- **AI guidance is easier to trust.** Connected answers and generated quizzes can show the institutional materials used as supporting evidence.
- **The journey remains usable offline.** Curated content, verified quizzes, scoring, explanations, and core recommendations remain available without the AI service.

## Personalized Ocean Learning

BluePath begins with an ocean-talent DNA diagnosis. The learner selects an age group, interests, goals, and current level, and the app builds a personalized profile that influences content, schedules, career suggestions, and AI guidance.

The experience is suitable for:

- Children and families discovering marine life and culture
- Students exploring ocean science, safety, ships, and ports
- Career seekers connecting interests with maritime occupations
- Adult learners preparing for training, qualifications, or professional development
- Educators and program operators organizing structured ocean-learning journeys

## Tier-Based Growth Journey

BluePath uses a consistent tier identity throughout the home screen, quizzes, results, promotion guide, and My Page.

- 🥉 **Bronze**
- 🥈 **Silver**
- 🥇 **Gold**
- 🏆 **Platinum**
- 💎 **Diamond**

A learner’s effective tier reflects the highest achievement earned through XP, promotion quizzes, and the Diamond qualification pathway.

## Promotion Quizzes with Meaningful Feedback

Every promotion quiz uses four-option multiple-choice questions. BluePath does not reveal whether an answer is correct immediately after selection. Learners answer the complete set first and then receive a final score and detailed review.

| Promotion | Requirement |
|---|---:|
| 🥉 Bronze → 🥈 Silver | 7 or more correct out of 10 |
| 🥈 Silver → 🥇 Gold | 9 or more correct out of 12 |
| 🥇 Gold → 🏆 Platinum | 10 or more correct out of 15 |
| 🏆 Platinum → 💎 Diamond | 16 or more correct out of 20, plus approved evidence |

After submission, the result screen provides:

- Final score and pass status
- The learner’s selected answer
- The correct answer
- A clear explanation for every question
- Supporting sources for RAG-generated questions

BluePath includes a verified 57-question offline bank and can also generate personalized quizzes through the Marine AI service. Invalid or incomplete generated questions are rejected automatically, and the verified offline bank is used instead.

## Source-Grounded BluePath Marine AI

The BluePath AI Agent combines the learner profile, current tier, promotion rules, and retrieved marine knowledge to provide focused guidance. It can help learners:

- Choose the next topic or video
- Build a study sequence for a marine career goal
- Understand maritime, port, safety, and ocean-technology roles
- Connect personal interests with NCS competencies
- Review promotion requirements
- Identify a practical next action inside the app

When connected, answers include supporting titles, organizations, and links so learners can inspect the basis of the guidance. When disconnected, BluePath continues with its verified local content and recommendation logic.

## Marine Programs, Events, and Learning Schedules

BluePath brings educational opportunities into the same journey as videos and quizzes. Learners can discover:

- Museum and institutional education programs
- Maritime officer and safety-training courses
- Marine movies, performances, concerts, lectures, and experiences
- Family and youth activities
- Qualification and professional-development pathways

Programs can be saved, added to the device calendar, and supported by learning or exam reminders. When a signed-in learner refreshes the app, newly managed videos, programs, events, and schedules are merged with the built-in offline catalog.

## NCS-Based Marine Career Discovery

Career exploration connects marine interests with practical competencies and workplace types. BluePath introduces roles such as navigation officer, marine-environment educator, marine-ecology interpreter, ship safety manager, and port-logistics specialist.

Roadmaps can include competencies such as:

- Position fixing and navigation watchkeeping
- Ship handling and navigation-equipment operation
- Emergency response and safety management
- Marine-environment communication and education planning
- Port operations, cargo flow, and logistics data use

This helps learners understand not only what a job is, but also what they should learn next.

## Diamond Achievement Pathway

Diamond recognizes achievement beyond ordinary quiz performance. Platinum learners complete an advanced 20-question assessment and submit evidence for:

1. A relevant marine certification or approved learning credential
2. A marine project, portfolio, research activity, or field experience

My Page displays the advanced quiz status, certification review, project review, and final Diamond eligibility.

## Learner-Focused My Page

My Page functions as a personal learning dashboard rather than a developer settings screen. It includes:

- Current tier, XP, and promotion progress
- Completed videos and learning history
- Favorites and saved opportunities
- Quiz attempts and best scores
- Account sign-up and sign-in
- Cloud learning-record synchronization
- Daily learning reminders and exam-calendar actions
- Guardian-consent status for younger learners
- Diamond assessment and evidence-review progress
- Profile editing and reset controls

## Account, Privacy, and Guardian Support

Learners may use BluePath offline without creating an account. Signing in adds cloud synchronization so learning can continue across devices. BluePath supports guardian-consent records for younger users, keeps learner-facing screens free of service credentials, and limits the profile information used for personalization.

## Content Quality and Administration

A dedicated administration experience helps authorized operators keep the learning catalog useful and current. It supports:

- Video, program, event, and schedule management
- Promotion quiz creation and editing
- CSV and Excel uploads with row-level validation
- Institutional knowledge uploads for RAG
- YouTube Data API synchronization
- RAG embedding updates
- Diamond certification and project review

Updates made by content managers can flow into the signed-in mobile experience without replacing the app’s built-in offline resources.
